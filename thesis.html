"Learning Deception and Counter-Deception Strategies in Adversarial Settings"

This research explores optimizing multi-agent interactions using reinforcement learning and vision-language models. The primary objective is to decode deception in artificial intelligence systems and develop robust counter-deception strategies.

The work involves:

  • Designing adversarial environments for AI agents.
  • Implementing reinforcement learning algorithms to train agents.
  • Incorporating vision-language models for enhanced decision-making.
  • Testing strategies in complex multi-agent systems.

Key findings:

  • Deceptive strategies significantly influence agent interactions.
  • Counter-deception mechanisms improve robustness in adversarial settings.
  • Vision-language models enhance context understanding and performance.
